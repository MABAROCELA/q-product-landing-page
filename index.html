NINA NATURE

Description

Reconnect to nature with the new clean and vegan limited edition Nina Nature Eau de Toilette by Nina Ricci. As crisp as a just-picked apple glistening with dew in the clear morning light.

Blending natural apple extract with jasmine and vanilla, this green floral scent immerses you in fresh and flourishing nature wherever you are.

Reinvented in glossy spring green, the iconic Nina apple-shaped bottle is deliciously fresh for this new Nina Nature limited edition.

CLEAN & VEGAN

The 91% natural origin formula features Nina's signature ingredient of upcycled Italian lemon made with fruits excluded from the food trade due to their appearance or size.

Rather than being thrown away, these lemons that do not meet the aesthetic standards of the food industry are repurposed in perfumery.

All of the fruit is used: the juice, the zest and also the pulp.

COMPOSITION

ALCOHOL DENAT., PARFUM (FRAGRANCE), AQUA (WATER), LIMONENE, HYDROXYCITRONELLAL, LINALOOL, CITRONELLOL, CITRAL, GERANIOL, COUMARIN, EUGENOL, BENZYL ALCOHOL

HOW TO USE

Step into the freshness of nature by spritzing Nina Nature into the air and walking through the fragrance.

THE FRESHEST APPLE

Infused with pure and sparkling freshness, Nina Nature transports you to the great outdoors with its green floral fragrance.

Like a fresh apple picked in the cool morning dew, this women's Eau de Toilette is filled with a clear, clean crispness. Natural apple extract blends harmoniously with delicate jasmine blossom, wrapped in the gentle warmth of vanilla infusion.

Bursting open with a peppy duo of natural Granny Smith extract and upcycled Italian lemon, Nina Nature starts with a vibrant and energizing freshness. This leads to a blossoming bouquet of floral sweetness at the scent's heart, composed of jasmine and lily of the valley. The base notes reveal a soft warmth blending vanilla and white musk to round out this fresh and smooth Eau de Toilette.
